simple steps to make chicken biryani recipe

perfectly cooked chicken biryani ia an art that not many can master. it requires precision,patience and lots of love.it a wholesome dish that witnesses perfect textured long grain rice with tender meat and choice of vegetables

cooking time:25mins

preparation time:20mins

serving time:5min

steps:

step-1

add some water boil the chicken
with salt and turmeric powder

step-2

prepare biryani masala paste for flavour and good taste
it is main step to make biryani tasty and spicy
grind coconut,cinammon,coriender, chilli, ginger-garlic together
and make the masala first

step-3

take one vessel and add 3 table spoons of yogurt and ghee
1-2 bay leaves,chopped onions mix well together,
had masala paste that we have already prepared
soak the rice for 3-4mins

step-4

last step is that to add water that is double of rice
add some salt and mix it well
and close the lid get 1-2 whistles
and the dish is ready to surve
